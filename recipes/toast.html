Toast Recipe

A delicious cheese and sujuk toast

Dont forget to wait your toaster warm up before taking action.

Ingredients

  • 2 slice of toast bread
  • 2 slices cheese of any kind
  • 4 slices of turkish style fermented sausage
  • 2 tomatos

Steps

  1. Heat up the toaster good.
  2. prepare 2 slice of bread and cheese and 4 slices of sujuk.
  3. Cover the toast bread and slightly pressure the breads.
  4. Use some butter on the bread and cook until cheese become a little fluid like.

Slice the tomatos however you like. Have a good meal
